# 1892 Washington Senators season

The **1892 Washington Senators season** was a season in American professional baseball. The team, which had played in the now-defunct American Association in 1891 as the **Washington Statesmen**, was purchased by J. Earl Wagner and moved to the National League for the 1892 season. In a split season schedule, the Senators finished seventh in the first half of the season and last in the second half. Overall, the team had a record of 58вАУ93, 10th-best in the 12-team National League.

## Preseason

The Senators held spring training in Savannah, Georgia at Bolton Street Park.
