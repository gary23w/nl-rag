# Apple cheese

**Apple cheese** (Lithuanian: *Obuolių sūris*, Polish: *Ser Jabłkowy/Ser Jabłeczny*) is a traditional Lithuanian and Polish dessert, made out of boiled or cooked apples sweetened with sugar. Originally it was made with honey, which was later replaced by sugar.

## History

Apple cheese originated in the Middle Ages. The oldest dated recipe was written by the cook in the house of Radvila *(polish Radziwiłł)*.

In 1907, the recipe for apple cheese was published in the Lithuanian magazine **Lietuvos virėja** from Lithuania's capital Vilnius.
