**1065 Avenue of the Americas** (also known as **5 Bryant Park**) is a 451-foot-tall (137 m) office building at 1065 Sixth Avenue between 40th and 41st Street in the Midtown Manhattan neighborhood of New York City. It was completed in 1957 and has 38 floors totaling approximately 680,000 square feet. Architectural firm Kahn & Jacobs designed the building in collaboration with Sidney Goldstone for the Union Dime Savings Bank. In 2007 the building underwent a $107 million renovation and in 2020 the entrance and lobby was refurbished.
