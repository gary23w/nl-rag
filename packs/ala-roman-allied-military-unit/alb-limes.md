# Alb Limes

The **Alb Limes** (German: *Alblimes*) is a Roman frontier fortification or *limes* of the late 1st century AD in the Swabian Jura, also known as the Swabian Alb. The Alb Limes runs for just under 135 kilometres from Rottweil (Latin: *Arae Flaviae*) in the southwest to Heidenheim an der Brenz (Latin: *Aquileia*), Germany, in the northeast.
