# 1915 Buffalo Blues season

The **1915 Buffalo Blues season** was a season in American baseball. The team, which did not have an official nickname but was generally known as the **Buffeds** the previous year, adopted the "Blues" nickname for the 1915 season. They were also known as the **Buffalo Federals** or **Buffalo Feds**. They finished 74вЂ“78, good for 6th place in the Federal League, 12 games behind the Chicago Whales. After the season, both the team and the league folded.
