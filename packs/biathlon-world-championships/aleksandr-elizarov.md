# Aleksandr Elizarov

**Aleksandr Matveyevich Elizarov** (also **Yelizarov**) (Russian: Александр Матвеевич Елизаров; born 7 March 1952) is a former Soviet biathlete. At the 1976 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck, he won a gold medal with the Soviet relay team, and an individual bronze medal.
