# 1893 Boston Beaneaters season

The **1893 Boston Beaneaters season** was the 23rd season of the franchise. They won their third straight and sixth overall National League pennant with a record of 86–43–2 (.667), 5.0 games ahead of the second-place Pittsburgh Pirates.
