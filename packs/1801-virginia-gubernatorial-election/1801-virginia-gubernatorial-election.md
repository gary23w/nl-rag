# 1801 Virginia gubernatorial election

A gubernatorial election was held in Virginia on December 10, 1801. The incumbent governor of Virginia James Monroe was re-elected.

The election was conducted by the Virginia General Assembly in joint session. Monroe was elected with a majority on the first ballot.
