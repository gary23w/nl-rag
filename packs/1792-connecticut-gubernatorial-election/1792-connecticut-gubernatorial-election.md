# 1792 Connecticut gubernatorial election

The **1792 Connecticut gubernatorial election** was held on December 1, 1792, in order to elect the Governor of Connecticut. Incumbent Federalist Governor of Connecticut Samuel Huntington won re-election as he ran unopposed.

## General election

On election day, December 1, 1792, Federalist nominee Samuel Huntington easily won re-election as he ran unopposed. Huntington was sworn in for his seventh term on December 2, 1792.
