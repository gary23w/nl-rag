# 1976 United States Senate election in New York

The **1976 United States Senate election in New York** was held on November 2, 1976. Incumbent Republican U.S. Senator James L. Buckley ran for re-election to a second term, but was defeated by Democratic diplomat Pat Moynihan. As of 2026, this is the last time an incumbent Senator from New York lost re-election to this Class 1 seat.

## Republican primary

### Candidates

- James L. Buckley, incumbent U.S. Senator since 1971
- Peter A. Peyser, U.S. representative from Irvington
