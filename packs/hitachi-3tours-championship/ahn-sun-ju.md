# Ahn Sun-ju

**Ahn Sun-ju**, or **Sun-ju Ahn** (Korean: 안선주, born 31 August 1987) is a South Korean professional golfer currently playing on the LPGA of Japan Tour. She led the LPGA of Japan Tour money list in 2010, 2011, 2014, and 2018.
