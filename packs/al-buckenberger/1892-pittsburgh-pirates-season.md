# 1892 Pittsburgh Pirates season

The **1892 Pittsburgh Pirates season** was the 11th season of the Pittsburgh Pirates franchise; their sixth in the National League. In a split season schedule, the Pirates finished sixth in the first half of the season and fourth in the second half. Overall, the team had a record of 80–73, sixth-best in the 12-team National League.
