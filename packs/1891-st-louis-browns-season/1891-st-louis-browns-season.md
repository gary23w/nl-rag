# 1891 St. Louis Browns season

The **1891 St. Louis Browns season** was the team's tenth season in St. Louis, Missouri and the tenth season in the American Association. The Browns went 85вАУ51 during the season and finished second in the American Association.

This was the Browns final season in the American Association. The league folded after the season, and the Browns moved to the National League where they remain today as the St. Louis Cardinals.
