# 7 and 7

A **Seven and Seven** or **7 and 7** is a highball cocktail, a mixed alcoholic drink containing Seagram's Seven Crown, which is a blended whiskey, and 7 Up, which is a lemon-lime soft drink. It is typically served with ice. It was one of the most popular drinks in the United States during the 1970s.

## Mixture

The following is a typical recipe for a 7 and 7:

- Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
- Add 1 shot (about 1.5 US fl oz or 45 ml) Seagram's Seven Crown whisky.
- Add 4–6 US fluid ounces (120–180 ml) 7 Up, to taste.
- Garnish with lemon or lime wedge (optional).

## In popular culture

In the 1977 film *Saturday Night Fever*, Tony Manero (John Travolta) orders a 7 and 7 while at the bar of a strip club.

In the pilot episode of The O.C., Ryan Atwood orders a 7 and 7 cocktail at a party but only takes a sip before Kirsten Cohen takes it away from him.

In "Mixology Certification" the 10th episode of season 2 of the series *Community*, when celebrating his 21st birthday, Troy Barnes (Donald Glover) orders a 7 and 7, which he says was his late uncle's favorite drink.

In the 1990 film *Goodfellas*, the first time that Henry Hill meets James "Jimmy" Conway, played by Robert De Niro, at an illegal casino, Jimmy orders a 7 and 7, asking Henry to "keep 'em coming."

In the 2018 film *BlacKkKlansman*, Laura Harrier as Patrice Dumas orders a 7 and 7 in the bar scene after the Kwame Ture speech.

Namesake of the song "7 & 7" from the 2003 album *Diamonds & Gasoline* by the Turnpike Troubadours and mentioned in the first verse.
