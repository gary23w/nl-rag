# 1804 Virginia gubernatorial election

A gubernatorial election was held in Virginia on December 7, 1804. The incumbent governor of Virginia John Page was re-elected.

The election was conducted by the Virginia General Assembly in joint session. Page was elected with a majority on the first ballot.
