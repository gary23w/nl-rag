# 1891 Pittsburgh Pirates season

The **1891 Pittsburgh Pirates season** was the tenth season of the Pittsburgh Pirates franchise and the first season under the "Pirates" name; their fifth place in the National League. The Pirates finished eighth and last in the National League with a record of 55–80–2 (.407), 30.5 games behind the first-place Boston Beaneaters.
