# 1960 AFC Youth Championship

The **1960 AFC Youth Championship** was held in Malaya.

## Teams

The following teams entered the tournament:

- Burma
- Indonesia
- Japan
- Malaya (host)
- Philippines
- Singapore
- South Korea
- Thailand
