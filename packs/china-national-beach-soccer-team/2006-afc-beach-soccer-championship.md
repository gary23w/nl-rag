# 2006 AFC Beach Soccer Championship

The **2006 AFC Beach Soccer championship** also known as the **2006 FIFA Beach Soccer World Cup qualifiers for (AFC)** was the first beach soccer championship for Asia, held in May 2006, in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Bahrain won the championship, with Japan finishing second and Iran winning the third place-play off, to claim third. The three teams moved on to play in the 2006 FIFA Beach Soccer World Cup in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil from 2 November – 12 November.

## Competing nations

- Bahrain
- China
- Iran
- Japan
- Philippines
- United Arab Emirates *(hosts)*
