# 1999 South Pacific Games

The **11th South Pacific Games**, also known as **Guam 1999** (Chamorro: *Guåhån 1999*), held in Santa Rita and Hagåtña, Guam from 29 May to 12 June 1999, was the eleventh edition of the South Pacific Games.

## Participating countries

The 21 countries that competed at the 1999 South Pacific Games were:

- American Samoa
- Cook Islands
- Fiji
- French Polynesia
- Guam
- Kiribati
- Marshall Islands
- Micronesia
- Nauru
- New Caledonia
- Niue
- Norfolk Island
- Northern Marianas
- Palau
- Papua New Guinea
- Solomon Islands
- Tonga
- Tuvalu
- Vanuatu
- Wallis and Futuna
- Western Samoa

Note: A number in parentheses indicate the size of a country's team (where known).
