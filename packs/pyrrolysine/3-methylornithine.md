# 3-Methylornithine

**3-Methylornithine** is an amino acid with the formula H2N(CH2)2CH(CH3)CH(NH2)CO2H. This amino acid contains two stereogenic centers, but only one stereoisomer (namely (3*R*)-3-methyl-D-ornithine) occurs in nature. It is produced from lysine by the action of the enzyme methylornithine synthase. The combination of lysine and 3-methylornithine, also mediated enzymatically, produces pyrrolysine, which, for some organisms, is a 22nd genetically coded amino acid.
