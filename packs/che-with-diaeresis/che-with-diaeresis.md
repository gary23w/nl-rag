# Che with diaeresis

**Che with diaeresis** (Ӵ ӵ; italics: *Ӵ ӵ*) is a letter of the Cyrillic script. Its form is derived from the Cyrillic letter Che (Ч ч *Ч ч*).

Che with diaeresis is used only in the alphabet of the Udmurt language, where it represents the voiceless palato-alveolar affricate /tʃ/, like the pronunciation of ⟨ch⟩ in "**ch**icken". It is the thirtieth letter of this alphabet.
