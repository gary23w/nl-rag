# 1787 Connecticut gubernatorial election

The **1787 Connecticut gubernatorial election** was held on April 12, 1787, in order to elect the Governor of Connecticut. Incumbent Federalist Governor of Connecticut Samuel Huntington unanimously won re-election as he ran unopposed.

## General election

On election day, April 12, 1787, Federalist nominee Samuel Huntington easily won re-election as he ran unopposed. Huntington was sworn in for his second term on May 11, 1787.
