## Twin towns – sister cities

Leipzig is twinned with:

- (Ethiopia) Addis Ababa, Ethiopia (2004)
- (United Kingdom) Birmingham, United Kingdom (1992)
- (Italy) Bologna, Italy (1962, renewed in 1997)
- (Czech Republic) Brno, Czech Republic (1973, renewed in 1999)
- (Germany) Frankfurt, Germany (1990)
- (Germany) Hanover, Germany (1987)
- (Israel) Herzliya, Israel (2010)
- (Vietnam) Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam (2021)
- (United States) Houston, United States (1993)
- (Poland) Kraków, Poland (1973, renewed in 1995)
- (Ukraine) Kyiv, Ukraine (1961, renewed in 1992)
- (France) Lyon, France (1981)
- (China) Nanjing, China (1988)
- (Greece) Thessaloniki, Greece (1984)
- (Bosnia and Herzegovina) Travnik, Bosnia and Herzegovina (2003)
