# 1791 Connecticut gubernatorial election

The **1791 Connecticut gubernatorial election** was held on April 14, 1791, in order to elect the Governor of Connecticut. Incumbent Federalist Governor of Connecticut Samuel Huntington won re-election as he ran unopposed.

## General election

On election day, April 14, 1791, Federalist nominee Samuel Huntington easily won re-election as he ran unopposed. Huntington was sworn in for his sixth term on May 10, 1791.
