# 1915 Chicago Whales season

The **1915 Chicago Whales season** was a season in American baseball. After not having an official nickname in 1914, the team officially became the **Whales** for the 1915 season. They finished the season with an 86вАУ66 record, placing them in a statistical tie with the St. Louis Terriers for first place in the Federal League. However, since the Whales had a slightly better winning percentage, they were declared the league champions.
