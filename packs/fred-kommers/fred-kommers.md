# Fred Kommers

**Frederick Raymond Kommers** (March 31, 1886 – June 14, 1943) nicknamed "Bugs", was an American professional baseball outfielder. Kommers played two seasons in the Major League Baseball. He debuted in June 1913 for the Pittsburgh Pirates and played 40 games for them over the rest of the season. In 1914, he split the season between two teams in the new Federal League, starting the year with the St. Louis Terriers and ending it with the Baltimore Terrapins.
