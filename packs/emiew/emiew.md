# EMIEW

**EMIEW** is a robot developed by Hitachi. Another version has also been made called EMIEW 2. EMIEW stands for **E**xcellent **M**obility and **I**nteractive **E**xistence as **W**orkmate. Two EMIEWs have been made, called Pal and Chum. Hitachi stated that Pal and Chum, have a vocabulary of about 100 words, and Pal exhibited these skills by telling reporters: "I want to be able to walk about in places like Shinjuku and Shibuya in the future without bumping into people and cars". Both EMIEWs have a top speed of 6 km/h (matching Honda's ASIMO) and can avoid obstacles.
