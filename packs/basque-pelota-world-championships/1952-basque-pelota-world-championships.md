# 1952 Basque Pelota World Championships

The **1952 Basque Pelota World Championships** were the 1st edition of the Basque Pelota World Championships organized by the FIPV.

## Participating nations

- Argentina
- Cuba
- France
- Italy
- Mexico
- Philippines
- Spain
- Uruguay
