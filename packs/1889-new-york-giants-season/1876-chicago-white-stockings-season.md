# 1876 Chicago White Stockings season

The **1876 Chicago White Stockings season** was the fifth season of the Chicago White Stockings franchise, the first in the National League and the third at 23rd Street Grounds. The White Stockings, as one of the founding members of the new National League, won the NL's initial championship during this season with a record of 52вЂ“14.
