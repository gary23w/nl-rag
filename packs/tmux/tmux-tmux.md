### Welcome to tmux!

tmux is a terminal multiplexer. It lets you switch easily between several programs in one terminal, detach them (they keep running in the background) and reattach them to a different terminal.

**Download tmux 3.7a here** (changes in this version).

See:

- the getting started guide;
- how to install tmux;
- the tmux(1) manual page;
- and the README.

For help and support:

- the FAQ;
- the tmux-users@googlegroups.com mailing list.

Report bugs by opening an issue. Notes on tmux development and ideas for contributions are here.
