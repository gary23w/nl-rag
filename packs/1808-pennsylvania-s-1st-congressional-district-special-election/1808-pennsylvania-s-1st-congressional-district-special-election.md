# 1808 Pennsylvania's 1st congressional district special election

A **special election** was held in **Pennsylvania's 1st congressional district** on October 11, 1808, to fill a vacancy left by the resignation of Joseph Clay (DR) on March 28, 1808.

## Election results

| Candidate | Party | Votes | Percent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Benjamin Say | Democratic-Republican | 7,598 | 55.7% |
| Charles W. Hare | Federalist | 6,046 | 44.3% |

Say took his seat on November 16, 1808.
