# 1,2-Butadiene

**1,2-Butadiene** is the organic compound with the formula CH2=C=CHCH3. It is an isomer of 1,3-butadiene, which is a common monomer used to make synthetic rubber, and is often co-produced with it. It is a colorless flammable gas, one of the simplest substituted allenes.

## Production

The C4-fraction of petroleum obtained by cracking and separated by distillation consists of many compounds, predominantly (75%) 1,3-butadiene, isobutene, and 1-butene. 1,2‑Butadiene comprises less than 1% of this mixture. It is partially purified by extraction with *N*-methylpyrrolidone. US production was 4,500–23,000 tons in 2005.
