# 2022 Women's Ball Hockey World Championship

The **2022 Women's Ball Hockey World Championship** (French: *Championnat du monde féminin de dek hockey 2022* or *Championnat du monde féminin de hockey-balle 2022*) was the eighth women's ball hockey tournament to be held as part of the Ball Hockey World Championship. It was organized by the International Street and Ball Hockey Federation in collaboration with the Canadian Ball Hockey Association and was held at the Place Bell in Laval, Quebec, Canada. The tournament began on 21 June 2022 and medal games were played 27 June 2022.

The Canadian women's national ball hockey team earned their sixth World Championship title after claiming a 3–2 victory over the Czech Republic in the gold medal game.
